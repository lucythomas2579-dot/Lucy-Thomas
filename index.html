About Lucy thomas

Lucy thomas is an international non-profit dedicated to improving outcomes for children through nutrition, education, and community programs. We partner with local organizations and publish transparent reports so donors can see where money goes.

Trusted — independent audits and public program reports. Impact-focused — measurable goals and clear outcomes.

Our Programs

Where donations go — transparent and impact-driven.

Education

School supplies, books, teacher support, and tuition assistance so children can stay in school.

Nutrition

Daily meals and nutrition programs to ensure healthy growth and better learning outcomes.

Health

Basic health screenings, vaccinations, and hygiene supplies to keep kids healthy.

Safe Spaces

Community centers and after-school programs that provide safe places to learn and play.
